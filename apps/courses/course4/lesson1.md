# Lesson 1: Introduction

## Defensive Measures in Cybersecurity

Just like how security attacks are present in the physical and digital world, so are the defenses that must respond to them. There are a **variety of defenses** that come in many different forms that help **protect** valuable assets. Whether it be **company** regulations or **defensive software**, every bit of security that can be implemented never hurts, it is important to always have a **variety** of defenses in different areas to stay secure.

In this course, you will learn types of **real-world** technical defenses, many of which are general concepts that will be applicable to any situation you face! Let's begin.

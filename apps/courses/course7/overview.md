# Course 7 - Advanced Attacks

## Course Overview

This course builds upon Course 3 by diving deeper into more sophisticated attack types. You'll learn about Advanced Persistent Threats, spoofing techniques, and interception attacks with more technical detail.

## Lessons

1. **Lesson 1: Introduction** - Overview of advanced cyberattacks
2. **Lesson 2: Persistent Threats** - Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs)
3. **Lesson 3: Spoofing** - Disguising identity and communication sources
4. **Lesson 4: Interception Attacks** - Man-in-the-middle attacks and eavesdropping

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will:

- Understand more sophisticated attack methodologies
- Learn about Advanced Persistent Threats and their characteristics
- Recognize spoofing attacks including IP, email, and DNS spoofing
- Understand interception attacks and how they compromise communications

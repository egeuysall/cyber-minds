# Course 8 - Risk Assessment and Management

## Course Overview

This course teaches you how to evaluate and improve your cybersecurity posture. You'll learn to identify risks, implement protective measures, evaluate their effectiveness, and continuously improve your security.

## Lessons

1. **Lesson 1: Introduction** - Understanding your cybersecurity position
2. **Lesson 2: Identifying Risks** - Cataloging vulnerabilities and threats
3. **Lesson 3: Protecting Against Risks** - Implementing security controls
4. **Lesson 4: Evaluating Your Measures** - Testing and monitoring effectiveness
5. **Lesson 5: Improve Your Security** - Continuous improvement based on assessments
6. **Lesson 6: Public WiFi** - Understanding and mitigating public network risks

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will:

- Learn how to assess your current cybersecurity position
- Identify and catalog potential risks and vulnerabilities
- Implement appropriate security measures
- Evaluate the effectiveness of your security controls
- Understand continuous improvement in cybersecurity
- Recognize the dangers of public WiFi and how to stay safe

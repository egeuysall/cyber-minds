# Course 2 - Cybersecurity for Individuals and Companies

## Course Overview

This course explores how cybersecurity needs differ between individuals and organizations. You'll learn about the unique security challenges faced by both groups and discover the actions they take to protect themselves in the digital world.

## Lessons

1. **Lesson 1: Introduction** - Individuals vs. Companies in Cybersecurity
2. **Lesson 2: Cybersecurity for Individuals** - Cyber-threats and protective actions for individuals
3. **Lesson 3: Cybersecurity for Companies** - Corporate security measures and frameworks
4. **Lesson 4: Mandatory Policies** - Legal requirements for data protection

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will:

- Understand the different cybersecurity concerns for individuals and companies
- Learn essential security practices everyone should follow
- Discover how companies protect themselves and their customers
- Become familiar with important data protection regulations (HIPAA, GDPR, PCI DSS, GLBA)

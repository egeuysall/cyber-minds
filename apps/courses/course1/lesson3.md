# Lesson 3: Cybersecurity Jobs

## What are Cybersecurity Jobs?

There are a diverse range of **Cybersecurity Jobs** all over the world! They play a crucial role in keeping all devices protected and safe.

### Popular Jobs...

- **Penetration Testers:** Legally hack systems to identify weaknesses and build enhanced protections to block hackers
- **Security Specialist:** Provide advice on cybersecurity management for an organization or company
- **Incident Responders:** Rapidly manage new issues related to cybersecurity as they rise
- **Cybersecurity Managers:** Lead and manage a team of cybersecurity professionals

**Cybersecurity Jobs** offer opportunities for **career growth** and **personal development**. In a world where cyber attacks continue to increase, cybersecurity jobs are not just careers; they are the **guardians** of our digital future!

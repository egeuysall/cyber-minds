# Course 1 - Introduction to Cybersecurity

## Course Overview

This course covers the fundamentals of Cybersecurity, exploring its purposes and the true meaning. You'll learn what cybersecurity means to different people and organizations, understand why it's critical in today's digital landscape, explore career opportunities in the field, and discover the core principles that guide cybersecurity practices.

## Lessons

1. **Lesson 1: Introduction** - What is Cybersecurity and why it matters
2. **Lesson 2: Statistics of Cybersecurity** - Why cybersecurity is more critical now
3. **Lesson 3: Cybersecurity Jobs** - Career opportunities in the field
4. **Lesson 4: The CIA Triad** - The three core principles of cybersecurity

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will:

- Understand what cybersecurity means to individuals, businesses, and governments
- Recognize the growing importance of cybersecurity in the digital age
- Explore various career paths in cybersecurity
- Learn the CIA Triad: Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability
